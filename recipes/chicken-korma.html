The Mighty Chicken Korma!

Description

This lovely Chicken Korma dish will keep you warm and satisfied on cold and dark evenings.

Ingredients

  • The ability to walk
  • The ability to see
  • The ability to use your hands
  • The ability to eat (that's an important one)

If you're confused, hopefully the "Steps" section below will clear up a few things.

Steps

  1. Walk to Indian takeaway place
  2. Order food
  3. Receive food
  4. Eat food

We did say these were recipes for the lazy adventurer after all. Feel free to level-up to ‘Seasoned Adventurer’ by skipping step 1, and ordering your food from a remote location.
